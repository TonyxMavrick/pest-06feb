About NightShield Pest Control

Our Story

Founded in 2010, NightShield Pest Control was born from a simple mission: to protect American homes and businesses from pest threats with integrity, expertise, and cutting-edge solutions.

Over the past 15 years, we've grown from a small local operation to one of the most trusted names in pest control across the United States. Our success is built on a foundation of customer satisfaction, continuous innovation, and unwavering commitment to safety.

Today, we proudly serve over 50,000 satisfied customers annually, backed by a team of certified professionals and state-of-the-art technology.
